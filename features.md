## Feature Analysis by Solution

### Diligent Boards

**Core features**
- Secure board pack creation, distribution, and management across devices
- Agenda builder with pre-read distribution and version control
- Native annotation tools for PDF materials with iOS/Android mobile apps
- AI-powered meeting minutes: auto-generates first draft from agendas, typed notes, transcripts, and board materials
- Voting and resolution capture with DocuSign e-signature integration
- Secure messaging that removes sensitive conversations from email
- Governance Cloud integration connecting boards, entity management, and GRC in one platform
- Entity management for multinational subsidiary structures (Diligent Entities module)
- Insider trading pre-clearance workflows and Section 16 compliance (Diligent Equity Effect module)
- Advanced committee reporting with pre-formatted risk reports on cybersecurity, climate, talent, audit

**Differentiating features**
- AI Board Member: AI assistant that summarises and compares board materials, builds agendas, drafts disclosures, and analyses peer trends
- Full Governance Cloud breadth: board portal + GRC + entity management + insider trading + ESG in one platform
- Used by 50% of the Fortune 1000; deep public-company feature set
- Peer benchmarking and shareholder activism monitoring tools

**UX patterns**
- Desktop and mobile-first responsive design; iOS and Android native apps
- Director-centric interface emphasising quick access to current materials
- Role-based access controls with granular folder permissions
- Offline access to board materials

**Integration points**
- Office 365 (Word, Outlook, SharePoint)
- DocuSign e-signatures
- Zoom and Microsoft Teams for virtual meetings
- SSO via SAML 2.0 / corporate IdP
- REST API via Diligent Developer Portal (developer.diligent.com)
- HighBond/One Platform API for GRC and workflow automation

**Known gaps**
- Complexity and cost create high friction for smaller organisations
- Some users report the UI feels dated compared to newer entrants
- Pricing opacity: enterprise contracts require negotiation; no self-service tiers
- Onboarding for new board members remains a manual, time-consuming process
- Integration depth outside the Microsoft/DocuSign ecosystem is limited

**Licence / IP notes**
- Fully proprietary; no open-source components exposed
- Patent activity around AI board assistant features likely but not confirmed publicly

## Cross-Cutting Feature Themes

### Table-Stakes Features
- Secure document repository with role-based access controls and folder permissions
- Agenda builder and board book / board pack assembly
- Meeting management: scheduling, pre-reads, annotations, voting
- Minutes generation and action item tracking
- e-Signature support (built-in or DocuSign integration)
- Mobile apps (iOS and Android) with offline access
- SSO / SAML 2.0 integration with corporate identity providers
- MFA and audit logs
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Secure messaging to replace email for sensitive governance discussions

### Differentiating Features
- AI-powered minute drafting and transcript generation (Diligent, OnBoard, Govenda, Aprio)
- AI report writer that improves quality of board materials (Board Intelligence Lucia)
- AI governance advisor / chatbot (Aprio ChatDPQ)
- Entity management for multinational subsidiary structures (Diligent)
- Insider trading pre-clearance and Section 16 compliance (Diligent Equity Effect)
- GRC integration (Diligent One Platform)
- Board effectiveness analytics and evaluations (Board Intelligence, OnBoard skills tracking)
- Flat-fee unlimited-user pricing (Aprio)
- Cross-board organisational view (BoardPro My BoardPro)
- European data residency and FINMA compliance (Sherpany)
- Digital circular resolutions (Sherpany, Convene)

### Underserved Areas / Opportunities
- **Open-source or self-hostable option**: Every major competitor is proprietary SaaS with opaque pricing; organisations with strict data sovereignty requirements have no open alternative
- **Integrated onboarding for new directors**: The transition process for new board members (orientation, document access, historical context) is manual in all platforms
- **Cross-platform regulatory filing automation**: Connecting board decisions to SEC EDGAR filings (Form 4, 8-K, proxy statement) is disconnected from board portals in most platforms
- **AI board composition and succession planning**: Only OnBoard offers skills tracking; none offer AI-driven recommendations for board gaps
- **Shareholder engagement integration**: Communication with institutional shareholders around proxy season is entirely disconnected from board workflow tools
- **Small-board and cooperative governance**: Nonprofit and SMB tools are significantly under-featured vs enterprise; nonprofit boards often lack even the basics
- **Real-time meeting facilitation**: Voting, Q&A, and engagement tools during live meetings are weak across the category
- **Regulatory deadline tracking**: Automated monitoring of jurisdiction-specific filing deadlines across multiple subsidiaries is limited to high-end enterprise tiers

### AI-Augmentation Candidates
- Minutes drafting from transcripts and agenda (already emerging, but quality varies significantly)
- AI summarisation of long board packs to surface key decisions and risks
- Regulatory change monitoring: alerting governance teams to new filing requirements
- Automated entity hierarchy visualisation from unstructured ownership documents
- Conflict-of-interest detection from director interest disclosures
- AI governance advisor for board best-practice guidance
- Action item extraction and owner assignment from meeting transcripts

## Legal & IP Summary

All analysed solutions are fully proprietary commercial SaaS products. No open-source board portal products with comparable feature sets were identified. Vendor AI features (minute drafting, summarisation, governance advisor) are proprietary and likely covered by trade secrets and potentially patents; no specific patents were identified in public searches. Diligent's Governance Cloud breadth represents a significant platform moat built through acquisition (Blueprint OneWorld for entity management, Equity Effect for insider trading compliance). There are no known copyright, licence compatibility, or patent concerns for building a new open-source competitor, provided original implementation. Feature lists and workflow designs are not copyrightable, though specific UI implementations may involve design patents.

## Recommended Feature Scope

**Must-have (MVP)**
- Secure board pack creation, distribution, and access (document management with role-based permissions)
- Agenda builder with template support
- Meeting minutes editor with action item tracking
- Voting and approval workflows
- Mobile-responsive web application (or native apps) with offline capability
- SSO / SAML 2.0 and MFA authentication
- Audit log of all access and governance actions

**Should-have (v1.1)**
- AI-powered minutes drafting from agenda and transcript
- AI summarisation of board pack documents
- D&O questionnaire module
- E-signature integration (DocuSign or built-in)
- Committee management (sub-boards, delegation)
- Governance document repository with version control
- Calendar sync (Outlook, Google Calendar)

**Nice-to-have (backlog)**
- Entity management for subsidiary structures
- Insider trading pre-clearance and trading window management
- Board composition skills tracking and succession planning
- Regulatory filing deadline tracker (multi-jurisdiction)
- AI governance advisor / chatbot
- Shareholder engagement and proxy management module
- Cross-board organisational hierarchy view
